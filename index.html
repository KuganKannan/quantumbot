About QuantumBot

QuantumBot is a chatbot by Kugan Kannan (4V) and Vighnesh Kumaramangalam (4N) designed to offer emotional support and assist medical students. It helps students overcome anxiety and depression, aiding them in passing international exams.

Features

Our chatbot provides emotional support and aids students in preparing for their NEET exams.
